The Best Apps to Make Money for 2024

by: Josue Caballero Sanchez | March 3, 2024

Apps are one of the most easily accessible ways to make extra money. All you need is a smartphone which most people already have. So if you are looking to make extra money, apps are definitely something to explore.

This list is made up of sites that are almost exclusively apps or work best on mobile. With these apps, you can use your phone to earn money and all of these apps are completely free and are available on both Android and ios.

1. Eureka

Eureka is a survey app that pays users to share their opinions and complete surveys. There are many survey apps out there but Eureka is definitely among the best. You can earn money by completing surveys, check-ins, daily polls, and claiming location bonuses. Eureka also has many different ways to cash out including PayPal, Bank Transfer, and a variety of gift cards. Eureka even has a referral program where you can earn money by inviting friends and family. This app gets a lot of bad reviews because of cash out issues, but if you cash out correctly you should have no issues. After cashing out in the app make sure to claim your cash out via the email they send to cash out properly.

Highlights:

• Multiple ways to make money

• Many different ways to cashout

• Low payout threshold

Approximate monthly earnings:

$10 - $125

2. Offerup

Offerup is one of the biggest mobile marketplaces for local buyers and sellers and is a great way to sell unwanted things or flip items for a profit. Offerup even offers shipping so you can reach more people. Shipping is easy as Offerup will provide a label and the customer will pay the shipping costs. Listing items is extremely simple and easy and only takes a few minutes. All you have to do is take a few pictures, input a description, and put a price on something to list it. Offerup also has great safety features like being able to message directly in the app and being able to view other people's profiles before selling or buying.

Highlights:

• Listing items is fast and easy

• Get paid instantly after selling

• Great safety features

Approximate monthly earnings:

$10 - $500

3. 1Q

1Q is a mobile research platform that pays users for their feedback. 1Q is unique because users don't take long-drawn-out surveys, they take short surveys and get paid instantly. surveys usually take 2 - 5 minutes and pay around $0.25 - $4. 1Q notifies you when a question is available and when you complete it you instantly get paid via PayPal. Not to mention 1Q also has an affiliate program where you can earn by inviting friends and family. The only downside to 1Q is that you have to wait to receive surveys and they can sometimes take a while, but 1Q is still a great app to make some extra money.

Highlights:

• Instant payouts

• Easy quick surveys

• Surveys are easy to complete

Approximate monthly earnings:

$5 - $20

4. Amazon Shopper

Amazon Shopper Panel is an app that lets you earn money that you can use to spend on Amazon or donate to charity. The way you earn money on Amazon Shopper Panel is by taking short surveys, submitting receipts, and enabling ad verification. Surveys usually take between 1- 3 min and pay out between $0.25 - $1 and you can earn $10 a month by uploading receipts. Earning rewards on Amazon Shopper Panel is really easy and even though rewards are only paid out at the end of the month and you can only cash out to Amazon, this is still a great app to make some money.

Highlights:

• Easy quick surveys

• Multiple ways to make money

• Made by a trusted company

Approximate monthly earnings:

$5 - $25

5. Google Rewards

Google Opinion Rewards is very identical to 1Q, as with Google Opinion Rewards you also take short easy surveys to earn rewards. Google Opinion Rewards sends you a notification when you have a survey available and gives you a time period to complete that survey. Usually, you have around 24 hours to complete surveys. Surveys are short and take around 1 - 2 min with the average survey taking less than 20 seconds, and paying around $0.25 - $1. You do need to wait for surveys and you also need a minimum of $2 to cash out via PayPal but Google Opinion Rewards is still a great app to make easy money.

Highlights:

• High payout rates

• No minimum to cashout

• Great user reviews

Approximate monthly earnings:

$5 - $20

These are the best apps that can help you make more money, each of these apps is useful and unique in its own way so hopefully, you were able to find something that suits your needs.
