The Best Websites to Make Money Online for 2024

by: Josue Caballero Sanchez | March 3, 2024

Making money online is a great way to earn extra income and many great websites allow you to do this. Most of the websites on this list will have you completing simple tasks that almost anyone can do, making them one of the easiest ways to earn money online and on your own schedule.

Whether you want to take surveys, complete studies, do freelance work or play games to make money, these are some of the best websites that will help you make money online.

1. Data Annotation

Data Annotation is a website where you can earn money by completing projects that they use to test and train AI. Projects are available 24/7 and you can work whenever you want from the comfort of your home. Projects can range from testing the quality of 2 AI chatbots to writing prompts for the AIs. If you are proficient in computer science you can unlock even more projects that can pay up to $40 per hour, but the pay depends on the project and usually ranges from $20 - $40 per hour. The only cashout option is through PayPal and it takes 7 days for the funds to be withdrawable, but this is a great website for people with busy schedules who want to work on their own schedule.

Highlights:

• Work on your own schedule

• pays up to $40 per hour

• Great user reviews

Approximate monthly earnings:

$300 - $1500

2. Freecash

Freecash is a website where you can earn money by playing games, completing tasks, signing up for new services, and taking surveys. They also have a daily and monthly leaderboard where you can earn up to $50 or $500 on the daily and monthly leaderboard respectively. Not to mention they also have one of the most rewarding referral programs where you can boost your earnings by inviting friends and family members to join. Freecash also has many different ways to cash out including PayPal, bank transfer, crypto, and gift cards. If you sign up using the button you will even get a free case worth up to $250, and if you earn $1 within 48 hours of signing up you will get three more cases.

Highlights:

• Multiple ways to make money

• Many different ways to cashout

• Great reviews on trustpilot

Approximate monthly earnings:

$100 - $500

3. Intellizoom

Intellizoom is a survey and product testing website that pays you for completing studies and sharing your opinions. Unlike other survey websites, IntelliZoom will pay a lot more for completing studies. Studies take around 5 - 20 minutes and the pay ranges from $1 - $10. If you are comfortable being on camera you can also take studies that require a webcam for higher paying studies. The only downsides to IntelliZoom are that you have to wait to receive studies, take screeners before each study, and the only payout option is through Paypal, but the positives greatly outweigh the negatives.

Highlights:

• High payout rates

• No minimum to cashout

• Studies are easy to complete

Approximate monthly earnings:

$20 - $100

4. Swagbucks

Swagbucks is very similar to Freecash as you can make money by completing various tasks like taking surveys, playing games, and signing up for new products. Swagbucks also has a referral program where you can earn money by inviting family and friends. Swagbucks has many different ways to cash out including PayPal and many different gift cards. Swagbucks is one of the oldest and most trusted sites and excels at having some of the best surveys for users. Swagbucks is one of the best ways to make extra money online and is an all-around great website.

Highlights:

• Great reviews

• Multiple ways to make money

• Multiple ways to cashout

Approximate monthly earnings:

$50 - $350

5. Usertesting

Usertesting is one of the best product testing websites with some great user reviews. Testers get paid for sharing their honest feedback on what they think about other companies' digital products such as websites. Tests usually require a webcam and microphone so if you are comfortable being recorded and talking to your device then this is a great website for You. Usertesting also has some great payout rates as you can earn as much as $60 per test. Usertesting does have some downsides like having limited tests, having to take screeners before each test to see if you qualify, and only being able to cashout via PayPal, but despite these downsides, Usertesting is still well worth your time.

Highlights:

• High payout rates

• No minimum to cashout

• Great user reviews

Approximate monthly earnings:

$20 - $200

6. Connect

Connect is a website where you can earn money by participating in tasks and taking surveys. Surveys on Connect average a pay rate of $10 per hour and you get to choose which surveys to take. Connect also has three great ways to cash out which include PayPal, bank transfer, and an Amazon gift card. Connect is a great survey site to make extra money since it is easy to use and has many great ways to cash out but it also has a few negatives. Registration takes a couple of days to get approved and it has a low earning potential, but it is still a great site to make extra money.

Highlights:

• Easy to take surveys

• Low payout threshold

• Multiple ways to cashout

Approximate monthly earnings:

$20 - $80

These are the best websites that can help you make more money online, each of these websites is useful and unique in its own way so hopefully, you were able to find something that suits your needs.
