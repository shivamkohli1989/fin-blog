The Best Survey Sites to Make Money for 2024

by: Josue Caballero Sanchez | March 3, 2024

Taking surveys is one of the easiest ways to make money online, and all you need is a smartphone or a laptop. Taking surveys is definitely worth looking into if you have some extra free time and want to earn some extra money.

This list of survey sites allows you to earn extra money using your spare time and all of them are excellent choices.

1. Swagbucks

Swagbucks is one of the oldest and most trusted survey sites and is one of the best survey sites out there. Swagbucks is more than just a survey site as it allows users to make money by completing various tasks like taking surveys, playing games, and signing up for new products. Swagbucks also has a referral program where you can earn money by inviting family and friends. Swagbucks has many different ways to cash out including PayPal and many different gift cards. Swagbucks excels at having some of the best surveys for users as they can even participate in product testing surveys and Swagbucks is an all-around great website.

Highlights:

• Great Reviews

• Multiple ways to make money

• Multiple ways to cashout

Approximate monthly earnings:

$50 - $350

2. Survey Junkie

Survey Junkie is one of the oldest and most trusted survey sites with some of the best user reviews. Survey Junkie shines not only because it allows users to take surveys but it also allows users to earn by product testing, participating in focus groups, and taking phone surveys. You can earn even more money by enabling their extension SJ Pulse which gives users more personalized surveys. Surveyjunkie also has many different ways to cash out including bank transfers, Paypal, and a Visa gift card. On top of all this Surveyjunkie also has a referral program which allows users to make more money by inviting friends and family.

Highlights:

• Easy to take surveys

• Trusted website

• Multiple ways to cashout

Approximate monthly earnings:

$30 - $200

3. Freecash

Freecash is another great survey site with tons of great reviews and a large community. Freecash is a website where you can earn money by playing games, completing tasks, signing up for new services, and taking surveys. They also have a daily and monthly leaderboard where you can earn up to $50 or $500 on the daily and monthly leaderboard respectively. This is a great all-in-one website and has many different ways to cash out including PayPal, bank transfer, crypto, and gift cards. Sign up using the button to get a free case worth up to $250, and if you earn $1 within 48 hours of signing up you will get three more cases.

Highlights:

• Multiple ways to make money

• Many different ways to cashout

• Great reviews on trustpilot

Approximate monthly earnings:

$100 - $500

4. Eureka

Eureka is a survey app that pays users to share their opinions and complete surveys. There are many survey apps out there but Eureka is definitely among the best. You can earn money by completing surveys, check-ins, daily polls, and claiming location bonuses. Eureka also has many different ways to cash out including PayPal, Bank Transfer, and a variety of gift cards. Eureka even has a referral program where you can earn money by inviting friends and family. This app gets a lot of bad reviews because of cash out issues, but if you cash out correctly you should have no issues. After cashing out in the app make sure to claim you cash out via the email they send to cash out properly.

Highlights:

• Multiple ways to make money

• Many different ways to cashout

• Low payout threshold

Approximate monthly earnings:

$10 - $125

5. Branded

Branded Surveys is another amazing survey site with lots of great reviews on Trustpilot. Branded Surveys has a nice and clean user interface which makes it very easy to use. Branded Surveys allow users to earn money by taking surveys, maintaining a survey streak, completing daily polls, and leaderboard bonuses. Branded Surveys also has lots of great ways to cash out including bank transfers, PayPal, gift cards, and donations. Branded Surveys also has a Branded Elite program that rewards users for maintaining a survey streak. Sign up using the button to receive a $1 welcome bonus to kick start your earnings.

Highlights:

• Easy quick surveys

• Multiple ways to make money

• Made by a trusted company

Approximate monthly earnings:

$5 - $75

These are the best survey sites that can help you make more money, each of these sites is useful and unique in its own way so hopefully, you were able to find something that suits your needs.
